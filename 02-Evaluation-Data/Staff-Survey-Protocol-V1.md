Goal: To measure the perceived efficiency and ease of use of the AI Protocol Tool.

Sample Questions:

Efficiency (Quantitative): "On a scale of 1 (Worst) to 5 (Best), how much time did the AI tool save you on this task?"

Ease of Use (Quantitative): "The prompt was easy to use and intuitive." (Strongly Disagree to Strongly Agree scale).

Value (Qualitative): "What is the single biggest benefit of using this tool?" (Open-ended).
